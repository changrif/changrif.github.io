SwampHacks

Spring 2017 - Ongoing

As a lead organizer for SwampHacks, I've led the organizing team for Florida's largest hackathon (projecting 600+ attendees in 2019) since the spring of 2017. I've managed everything from hackathon logistics to sponsorship, working on the budget, food, workshops, activities, judging and recruitment of volunteers, mentors and hackers. During my first year organizing the event, we grew to host 26 different schools from across the US, 82 submitted projects, and 532 participants. I also had a hand in managing the application process, dashboard, and live website/app used at the event. This year, I plan to continue to grow the hackathon, whilst setting up a firm foundation for the organization for years to come - building strong relationships with faculty at the University of Florida, the Gainesville community and our sponsors.
