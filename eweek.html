Engineers' Week

Fall 2016 - Spring 2017

As the Director of the Engineering Leadership Forum, I was responsible for coordinating the professional development workshop - showcasing different paths in engineering from a diverse set of panelists. Managing a group of three coordinators, we managed to redesign the event to attract a large group of students from across engineering disciplines at UF. We contacted speakers and panelists from across a wide range of backgrounds amongst corporate sponsors, Gainesville startups, UF faculty, and students. From the previus year, we grew the event's attendance from 15 to 90 students and decreased the budget by 70%.
